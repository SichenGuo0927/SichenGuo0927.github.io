Hi! I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Management Science at Research Institute for Interdisciplinary Sciences at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE), advised by Professor Chaolin Yang. I am currently a visiting student at University of Miami, advised by Professor Cong Shi

My research focuses on designing effective policies and algorithms for operations management problems including inventory management, supply chains, and dynamic pricing.

Currently, I am also exploring trust-aware algorithm design to enhance human-AI collaboration in operations management.
